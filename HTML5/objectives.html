Objectives

Strengthening Cyber Defence Capabilities

ECYSAP EYE enhances European cyber situational awareness, providing real-time intelligence to anticipate, assess, and counter threats.

Full-Spectrum Cyber Awareness

Develop a Recognised Cyberspace Picture (RCyP) integrating physical, virtual, and cognitive layers.

Mission Engineering for Decision Support

Enable scenario modelling and risk assessment for cyber operations.

Proactive and Reactive Defence Strategies

Automate cyber response workflows and threat anticipation.

Interoperability with EU and NATO

Align with FMN/NISP standards for seamless cross-border collaboration.

Distributed Cyber Intelligence

Enhance real-time information sharing between defence forces.

Agile Deployment

Reduce time-to-operation through early access releases and continuous user feedback.

By meeting these objectives, ECYSAP EYE ensures European cyber defence autonomy, reinforcing resilience, adaptability, and operational effectiveness in military missions.
