Events Participation

May 18, 2023 Madrid-Spain

Workshop ECYSAP, FEINDEF 2023

Workshop with Stakeholders on CSA: Strengthening cooperation with European R&D projects
  • Indra has organized the First ECYSAP workshop with Stakeholders at the Intenational Defence and Security Fair (FEINDEF 2023), bringing together leading experts and representatives of the most cutting-edge European cyber defence projects and entities in order to showcase the latest developments. EDF, EDIDP, PESCO and EU-funded projects shared the visions on R&D raised by NATO and EDA.

August, 2022 Vienna - Austria

Indra

ARES 2022 - 17th International Conference on Availability, Reliability and Security
  • Indra co-chaired the 3rd Workshop on Recent Advances in Cyber Situational Awareness on Military Operations (CSA 2022)

June, 2022 Bilbao - Spain

Indra

JNIC 2022 - VII Jornadas Nacionales de Investigación en Ciberseguridad (National Journeys of Investigation in Cybersecurity)
  • Indra participated on the JNIC 2022, talking about ECYSAP, which made a lot of people curious about the future outcome of the project.

November, 2021 Madrid - Spain

Indra

Workshop on Cyber Situational Awareness: building the future European capabilities for cyber warfare
  • The workshop organized by Indra brought main players of the defence sector such as the EDA, MCCD, Industrial stakeholders and universities to analyse European Cyber Situational Awareness technologies and the role of ECYSAP within the innovation roadmap. The workshop has been hold in conjunction with the International Defence and Security Exhibition (FEINDEF 2021).
