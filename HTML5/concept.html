Concept and Approach

A Mission-Centric Cyber Defence Framework

ECYSAP EYE redefines cyber situational awareness and decision support, integrating artificial intelligence, mission engineering, and advanced analytics to provide a full-spectrum understanding of cyberspace. Unlike traditional cybersecurity approaches, it analyses the electromagnetic, logical, and cognitive layers of the cyber domain, delivering real-time intelligence for military decision-making.

Advanced Decision Support Through Mission Engineering

By leveraging Mission Engineering (ME) methodologies, ECYSAP EYE enables:

  • • Scenario-based simulations to assess risks and opportunities.
  • • Proactive and reactive response strategies for cyber threats.
  • • Integration with digital twin technologies to enhance cyber operational planning.

Scalability and Interoperability for European Defence

ECYSAP EYE ensures:

  • • Seamless integration with EU and NATO defence infrastructures.
  • • Scalability to adapt to evolving threats and mission requirements.
  • • Real-time synchronisation with cyber intelligence networks.

This mission-driven approach strengthens Europe’s cyber resilience, empowering military forces with superior situational awareness and decision-making capabilities.
