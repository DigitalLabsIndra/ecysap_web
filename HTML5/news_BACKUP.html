December 14, 2021


ECYSAP Second Plenary Meeting took place as an online event with great involvement of partners and stakeholders


It came together all the consortium partners, European Commission and EDA project officers, as well as the participant member-states MoDs, into interesting discussions around the progress of ECYSAP within the first-year project and ahead. It deepened into the evaluation of the work performed and a forward-looking analysis of ECYSAP...

October 25, 2021


Indra is organizing the Workshop "Cyber Situational Awareness: building the future European capabilities for cyber warfare"


The workshop will take place at November 4th in conjunction with the International Defence and Security Exhibition (FEINDEF 2021). The Workshop is organized by Indra, has the goal to bring together main stakeholders of the defence sector into an open discussion point analyzing the current status, innovation paths and challenges European Cyber Situational Awareness technologies are facing around the defence ecosystem...



july 28, 2021


First ECYSAP plenary meeting


The first ECYSAP plenary meeting was held online with the participation of all the consortium members and representation of the European Defence Agency (EDA) and the Ministries of Defence (MoDs), heading the End User Advisory Board.



june 16, 2021


Indra will co-chair two workshops on Cyber Defence at ARES and ESORICS conferences


Indra is co-chairing the organization of two workshops in cyber defence to be hold in conjunction with the International Conference on Availability, Reliability and Security (ARES) and in the 26th European Symposium on Research in Computer Security (ESORICS) 2021...



february 12, 2021


ECYSAP Kick-off meeting held


The project kick-off has been held as a virtual meeting with the participation of all consortium members, with the endorsement of the European Commission.
