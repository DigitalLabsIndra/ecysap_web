News


July 20, 2024

Le Triomphe de Saint Cyr Coëtquidan


As part of the ECySAP project, Airbus Defence and Space Cyber participated in Le Triomphe de Saint Cyr Coëtquidan on July 20th. Le Triomphe celebrates the end of scholarship for French Army officers and welcomes new students. During the Innovation forum, Airbus presented solutions like CyberRange and ECYSAP to military staff and their families. Their participation demonstrated their capacity to address cyberspace, the fifth domain of warfare. The CyberRange is already in use for military operations and training. The ECYSAP solution is being developed with Indra, Leonardo, and Cybernetica, aiming for TRL7 readiness, in collaboration with the European Defence Administration and four Ministries of Defence.

ECYSAP Estonian Demostrator

This event further solidified the consortium's collaborative efforts and strengthened their collective capacities in advancing the project


April 16-18, 2024

ECYSAP Project Plenary Meeting


The ECYSAP project recently concluded its plenary meeting (from April 16-18) aimed at enhancing situational awareness in cyber defense. Participants from French, Italian, and Spanish Ministries of Defense, the European Defence Agency, and Consortium members Airbus, Cy4Gate, Cybernetica, Indra, and Leonardo convened to review progress, plan strategic initiatives, and schedule upcoming workshops and events.
"The meeting was highly productive, setting a clear pathway for our future efforts," noted Alejandro Campos, a project assistant.
Key highlights included progress reviews, agreements on strategic initiatives, and plans for workshops and events. The project aims to equip military operations with robust situational awareness tools to defend against cyber threats.


June 19-22, 2024

ECYSAP demonstration CWIX'24


From 19th to 22th of June, 2024, led by Indra with the collaboration of Airbus Defence and Space, the ECYSAP project was presented at NATO's CWIX 2024 exercise, held in June. Our primary objective was to enhance Cyber Situational Awareness and Recognized Cyber Picture capabilities. During the exercise, we successfully demonstrated our ability to receive alerts and incidents from SOCs, obtain information from C2 systems, send CTI information to SOCs, and provide mission impact assessments to C2 systems. This achievement highlights the interoperability and effectiveness of our system


April 15-18, 2024

ECYSAP demonstration in MILEX exercise


The ECYSAP project made a significant impact at MILEX'24, held from April 15-18, 2024. Our team, led by Pedro Ramón y Cajal Ramo, proudly demonstrated the latest advancements in defense technology and situational awareness, contributing to a robust discussion on the future of European military capabilities. ECYSAP's participation not only highlights our commitment to strengthening EU defense strategies but also reinforces our ties with key military stakeholders across Europe.

December 20, 2022

ECYSAP is at the forefront of the next European Defence.


The rise in cyber threats and the potential significant impact of a cyber-attack have highlighted the importance of providing military end-users with defensive and decision-making capabilities. Ambitious CSA project (ECYSAP) enters next phase. The European Defence Agency has recently signed a new implementing contract with the industrial consortium, led by Indra. To increase European CSA, EDA is supporting four EU States as the project manager of the ECYSAP project. The contract is an important step forward for the project, as it implements contributing Member States’ co-financing of the ECYSAP action launched under the EDIDP.


October 5-7, 2022

ECYSAP Third Plenary Meeting took place in Madrid, October 2022.


Indra has coordinated the organization of the first face to face plenary meeting in Madrid, arranged across three working days, from 5 to 7 October 2022. The event represented an excellent opportunity to strengthen bonds among the consortium members and the representatives of the MoDs and European Commission, all gathered for the first time since the beginning of the project since COVID-19 pandemic restrictions made it impossible until that time. The event was attended by all the members of the consortium. At the meeting, partners discussed the progress of the different tasks and the expected scope while the first outputs of the anakyses carried out on the European defence responses were presented

October 25, 2021

Indra is organizing the Workshop "Cyber Situational Awareness: building the future European capabilities for cyber warfare"


The workshop will take place at November 4th in conjunction with the International Defence and Security Exhibition (FEINDEF 2021). The Workshop is organized by Indra, has the goal to bring together main stakeholders of the defence sector into an open discussion point analyzing the current status, innovation paths and challenges European Cyber Situational Awareness technologies are facing around the defence ecosystem.
