Partners

Indra Sistemas S.A. (Coordinator) is one of the leading global technology and consulting companies and the technological partner for core business operations of its customers world-wide, with operations in over 140 countries.

Airbus CyberSecurity is a fully owned subsidiary of Airbus Defense and Space, providing reliable cyber security products and services. Its mission is to protect governments, militaries, organisations and critical national infrastructure from cyber threats, in compliance with the full range of measures required by national cyber agencies.

Leonardo is a global high-tech player in the Aerospace, Defence and Security. It designs and develops products, services, and integrated dual-use solutions for governments, Armed Forces and institutions, covering every possible operating scenario: air and land, naval and maritime, space and cyberspace.

Universidad Politécnica de Madrid (UPM) was founded in 1971 with the creation, development, transmission and criticism of science, technology, and culture in mind. UPM holds double recognition as a Campus of International Excellence.

Cybernetica is an R&D intensive ICT company with ca. 120 employees. It researches, develops and manufactures software solutions, maritime surveillance and radio communications systems; investigates and applies the theoretical and practical.

The University of Murcia (UMU) is a recognised research institution with a strong focus on cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and digital forensics. Its research groups specialise in cyber threat intelligence, critical infrastructure protection, and advanced encryption techniques. The university has played a significant role in EU-funded cyber defence projects, contributing to the development of novel methodologies for cyber resilience.

CY4GATE was founded in 2014 to meet an unconventional demand for cybersecurity. Listed on AIM since June 2020, CY4GATE was conceived to design, develop and produce technologies and products, systems and services.

Thales Hellas (THH), a subsidiary of the Thales Group, specialises in cybersecurity, secure communications, and digital defence solutions. The company provides critical infrastructure protection, encryption technologies, and cyber situational awareness capabilities to military forces. With a strong presence in the European defence ecosystem, Thales Hellas contributes to securing cyberspace operations and strengthening international cyber cooperation.

The Ministerul Apărării Naționale (Romanian Ministry of National Defence) is the central institution responsible for Romania’s defence policy, military operations, and national security. It oversees the Romanian Armed Forces and plays a key role in shaping the country’s cyber defence strategy, ensuring military preparedness against emerging threats in cyberspace.

The Austrian Institute of Technology (AIT) is a leading research organisation specialising in applied cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and digital defence technologies. The institute conducts high-impact research on cyber threat intelligence, risk assessment, and autonomous security systems. AIT collaborates closely with European defence agencies and industry partners to develop next-generation cyber defence solutions.

Enamor International (ENA) provides cutting-edge technological solutions for cyber defence, maritime security, and critical infrastructure protection. With extensive experience in working with NATO and the Polish Ministry of Defence, the company develops secure communication systems, cyber intelligence tools, and military-grade encryption technologies. Enamor International plays a key role in enhancing cyber resilience and interoperability within allied defence networks.

Eviden Technologies SRL is a Romanian subsidiary of Eviden, part of the Atos Group, specialising in advanced cybersecurity, digital transformation, and critical infrastructure protection. The company focuses on delivering cutting-edge cyber defence solutions, data analytics, and artificial intelligence (AI) applications for government, military, and private sector clients.

SPACE Hellas is a prominent technology company providing advanced ICT, security, and cyber defence solutions. The company specialises in secure communication networks, cyber intelligence, and IT infrastructure protection for military and governmental organisations. Space Hellas has a proven track record of participation in European defence programmes, offering expertise in network security, cyber threat intelligence, and digital forensics.
