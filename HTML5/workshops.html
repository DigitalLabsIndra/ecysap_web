Indra will co-chair two workshops on Cyber Defence at ARES and ESORICS conferences




16 June 2021

Indra is co-chairing the organization of two workshops in cyber defence to be hold in conjunction with the International Conference on Availability, Reliability and Security (ARES) and in the 26th European Symposium on Research in Computer Security (ESORICS) 2021.


As in the previous year the Workshop on Recent Advances in Cyber Situational Awareness on Military Operations (CSA) 2021 will be hosted by the ARES conference next August 20th, where Indra will have the chance to present three papers highlighting research activities carried out in relationship with the ECYSAP project. More information available in the CSA website.


Similarly, the 1st International Workshop on Cyber Defence Technologies and Secure Communications at the Network Edge (CDT& SECOMANE) will be hold jointly with ESORICS 2021 next October 8th. Indra will be chairing the event and presenting a conference paper as well. More information on the call for papers is available here.


Indra will not miss the opportunity to bring ECYSAP into stage by promoting the project endeavours within the cientific community.

This project has received funding from the European Defence Industrial Development Programme (EDIDP) under grant agreement Nº EDIDP-CSAMN-SSC-2019-022-ECYSAP.
